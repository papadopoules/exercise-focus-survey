This project is going to research the affect of physical activity on mental fog experienced during the day among students.
- Research Question 1 (RQ1):
 Students who exercise at least 3 times a week report higher levels of daily concentration. 
- Research Question 2 (RQ2):
 High-intensity training (e.g., gym, running) correlates with higher academic confidence compared to low-intensity (e.g., walking). 
- Research Question 3 (RQ3): 
Age is negatively correlated with the number of hours spent on physical activity per week. 


## Survey Questions
- Quantitative: How many minutes of physical exercise did you complete in the last 7 days? 
- Categorical: What is your preferred type of exercise? (Options: Weightlifting, Cardio, Team Sports, Yoga/Flexibility, None). 
- Quantitative: Rate your average level of mental "brain fog" during lectures on a scale of 1 (None) to 5 (Severe).
